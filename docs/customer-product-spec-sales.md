# Engineering Knowledge Assistant Sales Overview

Language: [EN](customer-product-spec-sales.md) | [DE](customer-product-spec-sales-de.md)


## Market Problem

Manufacturing organizations are rich in knowledge but poor in access.

Critical know-how exists in SOPs, maintenance notes, RCAs, safety procedures, quality standards, and vendor manuals, but teams still lose time searching, escalating, and re-answering the same questions.

That creates avoidable cost:
- slower troubleshooting
- longer onboarding cycles
- inconsistent shift-to-shift decisions
- avoidable downtime risk

## Product Positioning

Engineering Knowledge Assistant is a governed AI knowledge layer for industrial operations.

It is designed to turn approved engineering documentation into a trusted answer system with:
- source traceability
- confidence and knowledge-gap signaling
- multilingual retrieval support
- deployment flexibility aligned with customer security policies

## Core Value Proposition

Customers use this product to:
- reduce time-to-answer for engineering and operations questions
- reduce dependency on a small set of experts
- improve consistency of maintenance and safety execution
- identify documentation gaps before they create operational risk
- create a practical path from document sprawl to operational intelligence

## Why This Wins Versus Generic Chat

This is not open-ended internet chat.

It is purpose-built for controlled industrial content and returns:
- answers grounded in approved documents
- explicit citations for every response
- clear signals when the corpus is weak or incomplete

The result is higher trust and lower operational risk.

## Ideal Customer Profile

Best fit customers typically have:
- multiple production lines and distributed teams
- mixed-language workforces (for example English and German)
- high dependency on procedural compliance
- fragmented document repositories
- strong need for auditable and traceable answers

## Typical Business Outcomes

Within pilot and early rollout phases, customers should expect:
- faster troubleshooting cycles
- reduced repeated expert interruptions
- improved onboarding speed for newer staff
- better visibility into missing or outdated procedures

## Deployment Flexibility

The platform supports:
- Streamlit app on customer VM/server
- Docker deployment on customer infrastructure
- cloud-hosted managed deployment
- on-prem air-gapped deployment
- hybrid deployment with optional external AI backends

## Commercial Conversation Starters

Use these questions in discovery calls:

1. How much time do engineers lose each week searching for approved procedures?
2. Which recurring questions still depend on one or two experts?
3. Where do you currently lack confidence in documentation coverage?
4. Which site or line would deliver the strongest pilot ROI?

## Sales Narrative

Use this positioning statement:

Engineering Knowledge Assistant helps manufacturing teams convert approved documentation into a trusted operational answer layer so people can act faster, with more consistency, and with lower risk.
