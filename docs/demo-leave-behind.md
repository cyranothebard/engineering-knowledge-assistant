# Engineering Knowledge Assistant Demo Leave-Behind

Language: [EN](demo-leave-behind.md) | [DE](demo-leave-behind-de.md)


## What You Just Saw

Engineering Knowledge Assistant turns approved engineering documentation into a trusted, multilingual answer layer for manufacturing operations.

Instead of searching through folders and manuals, teams ask operational questions and receive:
- grounded answers
- source citations
- confidence or knowledge-gap signals

## The Business Problem It Solves

Most plants already have the knowledge they need, but it is fragmented.

This causes:
- slow troubleshooting
- repeat questions to experts
- onboarding delays
- inconsistent shift-to-shift execution

## Why This Is Different

This is not a generic chatbot.

It is designed for controlled industrial content and governed retrieval.

That means:
- answers are grounded in approved sources
- traceability is built in
- missing coverage is made visible through knowledge-gap detection

## Key Capabilities

- multilingual retrieval (English and German)
- citation-backed answer packages
- confidence and gap signaling
- lightweight usage and feedback metrics
- deployment options from VM and Docker to cloud, on-prem, and hybrid

## Typical Outcomes

Customers use this to:
- reduce time-to-answer on operational questions
- improve maintenance and safety consistency
- reduce dependency on tribal knowledge
- identify missing or outdated documentation earlier

## Ideal First Pilot Scope

Start with one site or line and a focused corpus:
- 25 to 30 vendor or equipment-related documents/excerpts
- 10 to 12 customer operating documents

## Next Step

Run a scoped pilot with your top 5 to 10 operational questions and validate:
- answer quality
- citation trust
- knowledge-gap visibility
- user adoption by role
